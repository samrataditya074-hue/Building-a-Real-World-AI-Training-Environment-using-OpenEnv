**Title: The Autonomous CEO — Executive AI Sandbox**

* **The Problem:** Modern reinforcement learning struggles to handle compounding, long-horizon strategic decisions where actions (like R&D or layoffs) take quarters to show impact, and "losing" simply means running out of cash.
* **Our Approach:** We built a fully OpenEnv-compliant, deterministic business simulation where an AI is forced to manage 8 complex strategic levers (pricing, hiring, budget, crises) under real-world economic pressures and random market events.
* **The Impact:** A highly explainable, multi-backend evaluation sandbox with dense reward continuous shaping and history-aware graders, allowing developers to test AI reasoning capacity while showcasing a beautiful, human-readable Gradio dashboard for stakeholders.
